1. Prepare Your Crystal

Place the crystal on the fixture and make sure the engraving area is stable and clean.

For flat crystal surfaces, no liquid is normally needed. For spherical or strongly curved crystal surfaces, immersion liquid may be required because of refraction.

2. Import and Process Photo

Open Falcon Design Space, import your photo, then use grayscale and invert filters when needed.
